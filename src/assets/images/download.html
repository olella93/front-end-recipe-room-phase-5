Tan Tan Ramen

45min
Tan Tan Ramen is a mouthwatering bowl of rich, savory broth with a spicy kick, perfectly complemented by tender noodles. Ground pork adds a satisfying texture, while a swirl of sesame brings a creamy depth. It’s a fiery, comforting bowl you won’t forget!

Meat
  • • 6 oz Ground pork
Produce
  • • 1 tsp Ginger
  • • 1/4 cup Green onions
  • • 1 tbsp Peanuts
Refrigerated
  • • 1 Egg, soft
Canned Goods
  • • 4 cups Chicken broth
Condiments
  • • 2 tbsp Sesame paste
  • • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
Pasta & Grains
  • • 4 oz Ramen noodles, fresh
Baking & Spices
  • • 1 tbsp Chili oil
  • • 2 Cloves
  • • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • • 1/2 tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • • 1 tbsp Sugar
Oils & Vinegars
  • • 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • • 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
